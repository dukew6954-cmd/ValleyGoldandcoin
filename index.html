Community Impact

We Donate to HopeKids & the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation

Every appraisal and every item we purchase fuels our commitment to families facing serious illness. Arizona Treasures proudly donates a portion of profits to HopeKids and the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation, helping provide magical experiences, research, and critical support for children living with life-threatening conditions.

Why We Give

Our Founder’s Daughter Inspires Our Mission

Our founder’s daughter lives with Cystic Fibrosis, a genetic disease that demands daily medications, therapies, and unwavering resilience. Walking alongside her through hospital stays and treatments has shaped the heart of Arizona Treasures. We understand how vital it is for families to feel supported, hopeful, and seen.

  • Daily respiratory treatments and clinic visits keep her lungs strong.
  • Exercise, nutrition, and specialized therapies help her stay active and joyful.
  • HopeKids events and CFF programs give our family community, resources, and hope.

When you sell with Arizona Treasures, you help fund the research, events, and resources that give children like our daughter reasons to hope.
